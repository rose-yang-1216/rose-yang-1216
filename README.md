# Rose Yang

Computational protein designer and PhD candidate in the DeGrado Lab at UCSF.

My research focuses on generative protein design and the exploration of synthetic protein sequence space using protein foundation models and structural prediction systems.

## Research Interests
- Generative protein design
- Biological foundation models
- Structure prediction and protein folding
- AI-guided therapeutic design

## Selected Work
- Discovery of a novel protein fold ("heliscrew") through deep learning–guided exploration of synthetic sequence space.
- Development of scalable pipelines for clustering and analyzing millions of predicted protein structures.
